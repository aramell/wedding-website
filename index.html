How They Met

Traveling frequently for work, attending grad school part time and fresh off knee surgery didn’t allow for much time to explore the dating scene, so Katie decided to go the efficient dating route and join Match.com (with a Groupon of course). Katie’s friends further supported this decision by crafting a profile for her one snowy day in January. Setting at least a 100 mile radius outside of Harrisburg (Katie was read to not meet someone from the Harrisburg area), little did she expect one of her first messages received would be from her future husband - who happened to be from Harrisburg.

Katie and Andrew’s first date was at the Happy Rooster in Philly the day after Valentine’s Day. Needless to say, the rest is history…

The Engagement

Andrew and Katie planned a trip to Nashville to visit friends. It just happened that quite a few of them were able to be in town that weekend. While killing time before dinner reservations, the group decided to check out “one more bar with a view” across the pedestrian bridge towards the stadiums. Side note – there are no bars across the bridge. Anyway, at sunset, Katie was excited to take a picture with Andrew given the beautiful view. Next thing she knew, he was down on one knee proposing, all of their friends cheering in the background. Andrew completely surprised Katie, which is a very difficult task if you know Katie. But very special thanks to friends and family who helped pull off a truly memorable moment.
